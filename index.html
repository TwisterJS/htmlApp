TwisterJS: Teaching kids to code

This project is managed by Carmalou. The goal of this project is to expose under-privileged young children to technology. This is being done through apps which will be available to download through the App Store and Google Play.

TwisterJS strives to give opportunity to children who would not otherwise have any. As such, everything here is open source.

Please use it to help those in your community.
